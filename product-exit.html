What is Exit?

Exit is a handy tool for car rental services. It allows you to keep track of your vehicles, manage your rental company, and much more. As we mentioned, you can manage your rental company and what's important, without being in It. It works with a ATM on the parking lot and all the customers have to do is to pick a car, pay and they are good to go. The ATM communicates with your device, and you instantly see what vehicles have been picked, which one are rented already, and which one are still unused. It is simple to use.

Why Exit?

It has much great features. It is simple and fun to use, and what's most importantly, It saves your time! One of many great features, is that It gives you an opportunity to design and customize your parking lot. You can add parking spaces, color and number them, so you can easily keep track of your vehicles. You can see the financial status. Another great feature is managing. Data, transactions and car statuses are updated every second, so you will be informed every time when a car was rented.

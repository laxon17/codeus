What is Aria?

Aria is a multi-purpose application for restaurants,pubs, bars etc. It's one of our first projects ever. It has a well designed user interface, which gives you a lot of options. Your waiters and other staff can learn how to use Aria in a matter of minutes. It’s simple, easy and fast. The communication of waiters, bartenders and kitchen staff is quick and automated. Waiters can take oders directly from the table, and send it to the bar/kitchen without any delay! Manage your pub, bar or restaurant from anywhere in real time!

Why Aria?

Create multiple restaurant sectors and customize table layout. Name and color code tables. Easily track their availability and transactions. Aria keeps track of your inventory for you. Quickly add, receive, and modify inventory. Save more time for things that are more important. See when your employees clock in and out from anywhere. Track and manage them easily. Track currently open and paid orders, current sales data and cash flow from anywhere. Employees clock in and clock out of shifts on Aria application so you know exactly how many hours they work, making shift management and payroll really easy.
